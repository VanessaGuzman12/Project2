Mortality in Mexico

In this graph we can see that the main cause of death in Mexico in 2020 was heart disease (251k), followed by Diabetes Mellitus (167k), but in position number 6 we have external causes (48k) that can be caused by firearm or knives that could be related to the drug war started in 2006.

As can be seen, these differences recorded in the mortality trends of women and men allow us to assert that the called "over-male mortality" has increased, which will have an important impact on the composition of the demographic pyramid of the country in the next years.

As we can see in this graph, both men and women die between 60-75 years of age, however, from 5 to 15 years of age, the decrease in the number of deaths is shown, which could tell us that the health system and the programs focused on this age group work.
